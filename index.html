Luxury Baked Goods

What we're about...

Decadance Bakery has always been a family-run business. Starting in a modest kitchen back in 1989, we strove to delivery only the highest quality baked goods to our loyal customers who lived in the area. Today we operate in 5 locations in the greater Western Cape province, with further plans for expansion!

A cup of coffee is not just a cup of coffee to us. Our expertly trained barristas take personal pride in delivering only the freshest beans, roasted to absolute perfection.We are very strict about quality control within our supply chain, and we belive the evidence is in the cup!

Our artisanl breads are what made is famous. Whether it's a sourdough or rye you're looking for, we will strive to deliver. So why not pop on down and reqard yourself with a fresh coffee and one of our delicious bakery goodies.

What we're famous for.

Roasted Coffee

Choose your bean. We stock up to ten varieties of carefully chosen coffee beans. Whether it's Kenyan Musk, or Kalahari Dark Roast, we've got it on the brew.

Our homely breads

Bread, glorious bread! Where would we be without it? There's nothing more comforting than that smell of freshly baked bread out of the oven. We keep things diverse, but also concentrate on getting the basics spot on. We roduce a fantastic sourdough (for which we're kinda famous for), but also produce beautifully dark, rich ryes. Come visit us for a free sample!

Artisanal muffins

Blueberry cheesecake? Lemon Poppy? Yup, we have em, plus a whole lot more. There's nop better way to start your day with a deliciously decadent muffin. And we're not stingy on the size either! These beauties are always availble and are on special during the morning rush between 6am and 9am.
